## Why Budget Earbuds Matter for Students

Whether you're grinding through lectures, drowning out your noisy hostel corridor, or just vibing to music during long commutes — a good pair of earbuds is a student essential.

The problem? Most earbuds at ₹1000 used to be... trash. But 2026 is different. Chinese audio brands and Indian budget labels have absolutely destroyed the price-to-performance game.

**We tested 20+ earbuds under ₹1000 over 4 weeks.** Here are the only 5 that actually deserve your money.

## FAQ

**Q: Are ₹1000 earbuds worth buying?**
A: Absolutely. The budget earbud market in India has improved dramatically. Brands like boAt, Realme, and Noise offer impressive specs at ₹1000 that rivals ₹3000+ products from a few years ago.

**Q: Which earbuds are best for online classes and calls?**
A: The Realme Buds T100 has the best microphone quality in this list, making it ideal for online classes and calls.

**Q: Do these earbuds work with iPhones?**
A: Yes, all Bluetooth earbuds work with iPhones. However, ecosystem features like instant pairing may not work.

**Q: How long do budget earbuds last?**
A: With proper care (storing in case, avoiding extreme heat), budget earbuds typically last 1.5–2 years.
