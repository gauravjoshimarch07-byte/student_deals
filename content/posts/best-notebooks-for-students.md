## How to Choose the Right Notebook

**By subject type:**
- Engineering/Science → Oxford A4 or Classmate 6-subject
- Commerce/Law → SIPP Long or Classmate single-subject
- Arts/Humanities → Any spiral notebook works well

**By pen type:**
- Ballpoint → Any notebook works
- Gel pen → Paperkraft or Rhodia (90 gsm)
- Fountain pen → Rhodia or Lexi Hardbound only

## FAQ

**Q: Which notebook is best for Class 12 board exams?**
A: The Classmate Pulse 6 Subject is perfect — it keeps all 6 subjects organized in one place and has enough pages for full revision notes.

**Q: Are expensive notebooks worth it for students?**
A: Usually no, unless you're serious about note-taking aesthetics. The Classmate 6-subject at ₹89 will serve 90% of students perfectly well.

**Q: What GSM paper should I look for in a notebook?**
A: For daily use, 65–70 gsm is fine for ballpoint/gel pens. If you use multiple highlighters or markers, go for 80–90 gsm to prevent bleed-through.
